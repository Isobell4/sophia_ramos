Sinigang na Baboy Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1 pack sinigang mix (tamarind or guava)
  • 1 large onion,Tomatoe chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut into 1-inch pieces
  • 1 cup kangkong (water spinach), washed and cut into 2-inch pieces
  • 1 cup long green chili peppers, sliced (optional)
  • 1 Sinigang mix
  • 1 cup Water
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  • In a large pot, combine the pork belly, sinigang mix, onion, garlic, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 25 minutes, or until the pork is tender.
  • Add the green beans and kangkong. Simmer for 5 minutes, or until the vegetables are cooked through.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve hot with rice.
